Ocula Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Overview

Ocula is a privacy-first AI assistant that runs entirely on your device. Your data stays on your phone — we do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data Processing

All AI processing (text generation, image analysis, voice recognition) happens locally on your device using on-device models. No data is sent to cloud servers for AI processing.

Permissions We Request

  • Camera: Used for real-time image analysis and document scanning. Photos are processed on-device and never uploaded.
  • Microphone: Used for voice input via speech-to-text. Audio is processed on-device.
  • Contacts: Used to answer queries about your contacts (e.g., "What's John's number?"). Contact data stays on your device.
  • Calendar: Used to answer queries about your schedule. Calendar data stays on your device.
  • Photos: Used for image indexing and visual search. Photos are processed and indexed locally.
  • Storage: Used to store AI model files and app data on your device.
  • Notifications: Used for background task completion alerts.

Data Collection

We do not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Conversation history (stored only on your device)
  • Photos, contacts, or calendar data
  • Location data

Network Access

Internet access is used only for:

  • Downloading AI model files (one-time, on first launch or upgrade)
  • Checking for app updates

No personal data is transmitted over the network.

Data Storage

All data (conversations, indexed content, AI models) is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. Data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

Ocula does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Ocula is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
